Salsa

Ingredients

  • 2 jalapenos
  • 5 verde habaneros
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 2 green bell peppers
  • 2 jalapenos

  • apple cider vinegar
  • salt
  • paprika
  • black pepper
  • olive oil
  • beet juice

Directions

  1. toss veggies in olive oil
  2. roast until brown
  3. let sit overnight in the oven for no reason
    1. Take a milk carton
    2. saw off the top
    3. pour in the roasted veggies
    4. take your hand blender
    5. blend.
  5. mix in salt, vinegar, paprika, and black pepper (and beet juice)
  6. blend
  7. garnish and serve
